About ClearSum

ClearSum is a small set of free, no-signup financial calculators: loan payoff, compound interest, savings goals, mortgage refinance breakeven, debt payoff comparison, and emergency fund planning.

Every formula is a standard, well-established financial calculation (the same amortization and compound-interest math used by banks and financial planners) — nothing proprietary, nothing hidden. We built it because most calculator sites bury the math in ads and popups; this one doesn't.

ClearSum does not provide personalized financial advice. The numbers are estimates based on the inputs you provide — always confirm important decisions with a licensed financial advisor.
